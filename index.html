About Yunis

Hi Nonolians, my name is Eunice Gabrielle Tabanao. I grew up in a broken family, and as the panganay or eldest child, I carry a lot of responsibility. My experiences have shaped me to be strong, independent, and caring toward my siblings. Even though life has been challenging, I strive to turn those challenges into motivation to do better and support my family


Life has not been easy for me, but I carry within me a strength that many will never understand. As the panganay, I have learned to stand tall even when the world feels heavy. Though my family is broken and my parents have built new lives, I am not defined by absence — I am defined by resilience. My grandmother’s love has been my anchor, and my own courage has been my guide. I may not have met my parents in person, but that does not make me less worthy of love, belonging, or success. My story is unique, and it proves that even in pain, hope can grow. I am not a victim of my circumstances; I am the author of my future. Every challenge I face is shaping me into someone stronger, wiser, and more compassionate. I choose to rise above the hurt. I choose to believe in myself. I choose to create a life filled with meaning, joy, and purpose. Being the panganay means I carry responsibility, but it also means I carry the power to inspire. My journey may be different, but it is mine — and I will walk it with pride, courage, and faith
